What I Do

At Biostat AI, we harness the synergy of biology, statistics, and artificial intelligence to:

  • Data Analysis: Analyze large datasets from biological experiments to discover underlying patterns and correlations.
  • Predictive Modelling: Develop predictive models for biological phenomena, from gene expression patterns to disease outcomes.
  • Simulation and Visualization: Use AI-driven simulations to predict biological events and visualize complex biological data for easier interpretation.
  • Custom AI Solutions: Design tailor-made AI algorithms suited to your specific biological questions and datasets.
  • Collaborative Research: Partner with biological researchers and entities for collaborative studies, ensuring the application of the latest statistical and AI techniques.

Who I Am

Biostat AI is a team of interdisciplinary experts, where biologists, data scientists, and AI specialists converge. Our combined experience spans decades, ensuring that we approach every biological query with deep domain knowledge, while also employing the latest techniques in statistics and AI.
